Terms & Conditions

1. General Terms

Welcome to Bite Box! By accessing or using our website and services, you agree to comply with and be bound by these terms and conditions. Please read them carefully before placing an order.

2. Ordering & Payment

  • All orders placed on Bite Box are subject to product availability.
  • We currently accept cash on delivery (hand-to-hand payment) only.
  • Prices are subject to change without prior notice.
  • Customers must provide accurate delivery details (address, contact number) to avoid delays.
  • For tiffin services, monthly subscriptions must be paid in advance via cash payment.

3. Delivery Policy

  • Delivery times are estimates and may vary based on location and demand.
  • We deliver within our specified service areas only.
  • For tiffin services, delivery times will be agreed upon at the time of subscription.
  • If you're not available at the time of delivery, we will attempt to contact you to arrange an alternative time.

4. Cancellation & Refunds

  • Food orders can be cancelled up to 30 minutes before the scheduled delivery time.
  • Tiffin service subscriptions require 12 hours notice for cancellation or changes.
  • If an item is unavailable, we will notify you and offer a replacement or refund.
  • Refunds (if applicable) will be processed in cash upon return of the product or during next delivery for tiffin services.

5. Food Quality & Safety

  • We maintain the highest standards of food hygiene and safety.
  • Please inform us immediately if you have any food allergies or dietary restrictions.
  • We reserve the right to refuse service if we believe a customer has health conditions that may be affected by our food.

6. User Responsibilities

  • You must be at least 18 years old to place an order.
  • Any misuse of the website (fraud, false orders) will result in legal action.
  • We reserve the right to refuse service to anyone for any reason.

7. Changes to Terms

Bite Box may update these terms at any time. Continued use of the website means you accept the changes.

Privacy Policy

At Bite Box, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Data: Name, phone number, delivery address, and payment details (if applicable).
  • Tiffin Service Data: Dietary preferences, regular delivery schedule, special instructions.
  • Non-Personal Data: Browser type, IP address, and cookies for website analytics.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To process and deliver your orders.
  • To manage your tiffin service subscription and preferences.
  • To improve our services and customer experience.
  • To send order updates and promotional offers (if you opt-in).

6. Your Rights

  • You can request deletion of your data, subject to legal requirements.
